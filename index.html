About RC Traders

Your Trusted Source for Genuine Imported Spare Parts

RC Traders (Pvt) Ltd is a registered supplier of genuine, quality automotive spare parts operating from Hendala, Wattala. For over 3 years we've supplied body parts, lighting components and accessories — sourced directly from Japan and Australia — to workshops and individual customers across the region.

  • 100% genuine parts, never counterfeit or low-grade copies
  • Direct import network across Japan and Australia
  • Serving workshops, garages and individual vehicle owners
  • Registered company — Reg. No. PV 00302151
